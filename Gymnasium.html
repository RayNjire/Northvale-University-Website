GYMNASIUM

The Fitness Center, located in the Student Center is definitely an original setting compared to other normal school gyms. It is situated outside the school compound and is not only used by students, it is also available to all other citizens but at a price. The pay is $33/month or $12/week or $5/day whichever you prefer; moreover you can be issued a personal trainer that will take your health and exercising experience to a whole different level.

There are all basic free weight machines. In the first room there are Squat racks, benches, dumbbells, etc. There is a large cable machine in the middle of the front room as well as Hammer strength shoulder and chest machines. In the second room there are 10 treadmills, 4 elliptical machines, 2 Stairmasters, and about 5 different types of ab machines. There are also some other odd machines: roman chair, lower back extension/oblique crunch bench, and a few others. In the third room there are all of the leg machines, another Hammer strength bench machine (targeting a decline similar exercise), and a grip machine. 2 squat machines, a squat rack, 2 leg press machines, leg extension, leg curl, and calf machines as well. And finally, in the fourth room there are a couple Hammer strength shoulder machines, a shrug machine, several bicep/triceps machines, several back pulley machines, a T-bar row machine, some Hammer strength back machines, a heavy bag and speed bag.

Mirrors have been strategically placed everywhere for personal admiration and etcetera. Dumbbells go all the way up to 180lbs. This is definitely the best gymnasium you can get to within a thousand mile radius. You’ll love it the moment you walk in.
