HOSTELS

Our school offers a prestigious hostelling experience famous in the folklore of the hostel industry. All rooms are fully equipped with ensuite bathrooms and time controlled, energy saving air-conditioners. There is also an in-house bar & lounge with a wide screen TV, cheap drinks, and a vibrant atmosphere. Combined, this provides a great opportunity to make friends with fellow students.

Our hostel has become one of the most popular hostels in Boston, too bad it’s only accessible to students and isn’t kept for online booking and all that jazz. Not only do we offer energy-saving air-conditioning, we also have heating units as well as bathrooms in every room. Sleeping experience has never been better until you’ve had a go at the luxurious beds we have set up for you.
