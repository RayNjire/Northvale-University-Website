KITCHEN

Most kitchens have functional interiors and this usually means lots of storage space, an airy interior with plenty of space to move around, large counter and work surfaces and also a chic and beautiful accent pieces. Kitchen islands solve most of the concerns enumerated above. They provide quite a lot of storage space inside all the drawers and shelves integrated in their design and they are also great as work surfaces. But our kitchen island has one more element that makes it truly stand out; beautiful lighting fixtures.


Our stylish and elegant kitchen island features matching industrial-style pendant lamps. The kitchen is a great place to include industrial lighting fixture designs. It has the simplicity and essential beauty that it needs to stand out.


Traditional white kitchen with a large island and antique industrial-style lighting Industrial-style pendant lamps have proven to be a perfect choice for our kitchen. They have simple designs and this is an essential detail in the kitchen. In here there’s really no room for unnecessary decorations or accessories. Then there’s also the fact that an industrial-style pendant lamp is a perfect element to be placed above the kitchen island. It complements its compact design and functional look. As in the case of any style, there are plenty of designs to choose from, but we decided to go with the best.
