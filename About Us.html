ABOUT US

The Northvale University of Technology is a private Ivy League University dedicated to hi-tech development and public enhancement technology-wise. The current Building was constructed between 1841 and 1846 along Arlington Avenue in Boston Massachusetts and is one of the oldest schools in the United States of America.

Having barely survived the second world war, (severe damage was inflicted on the ancient establishment), it was brought up from the dirt by Professor Gareth Vale from Boston and Sir Leonardo Thorne II from the United Kingdom and by 1940 the school was up and running in a few years’ time and the technology shared by these two developed countries are what have helped raise the school's well approved regards.

The School is now one of the most advanced places in the world. Scientists such as Steve Hawking and Ciara Pitts use the facilities in our school for research and occasionally motivate both students and lecturers in talks and organised seminars. The school offers a broad-based, multi-displinary education with emphasis on looking for a way to travel through the space time continuum and instant matter transfer.
