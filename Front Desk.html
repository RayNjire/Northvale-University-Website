FRONT DESK

The admission office is the school’s first and last hope of visual impressions so when it comes down to interior designing and responsible guidance, our front administrative hall is the place to be. The Front Office and is the most vital part of the Northvale University. The Front Office is in constant contact with guests, and has the most diverse operating exposure within the school. Our ideal Front Office associates are passionate about customer service, wear welcoming smiles, and literally emit assuring politeness. This is just one of the many things that this school has to offer.


Our associates have a keen intuition that allows them to anticipate needs and exceed expectations. These associates have excellent communication skills, can multi- task, are team-oriented, and work diligently to resolve issues when they arise. Furthermore the one most unnoticeable things, yet crucial indeed is that the secretaries’ desks are strategically placed in such a manner that overcrowding for personal attention is almost impossible.


As we all know, the secretaries are responsible for helping us make the very best possible. As keys to our guest satisfaction drive this department is critical to the continued success of our school, our brands, and our company as a whole.


In the waiting room, newspapers, school articles, and even imported magazines from all over the world can be found either in the book shelf or on the beautiful round glass tables right in front of the seats. A flat screen TV with never ending comical gold playing for your entertainment is situated right at the front of the hall.


You might be admitting your child into the school or you might have some burning questions or complaints, our friendly and flexible staff will be happy to help in whatever way that they can because it is people like them that make school services memorable for everyone and for the right reasons.
